Key Issues Compromising Performance

Critical Cash Flow Risk

A significant data governance failure means nearly half of all revenue is at high risk of payment delay or denial. This analysis, based on over 30,000 patient episodes, reveals a critical vulnerability that directly impacts cash flow and organizational stability.

Unrealized Hospital Capacity

Operational friction, especially in the patient discharge process, results in thousands of lost bed-days annually. This represents valuable, paid-for capacity that is unavailable for patient care, severely limiting throughput and access.

Concentrated Prosthesis Expenditure

A small cohort of high-cost surgical episodes accounts for a disproportionately large share of total hospital expenditure. This cost concentration is primarily driven by highly variable and expensive prosthesis charges within a few specific Diagnosis-Related Groups (DRGs), highlighting a key area for targeted cost control.
